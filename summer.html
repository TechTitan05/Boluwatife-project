SUMMER FISH BOWL

main spirit

Cordials

FLAVOR

Sweet

skill level

Beginner

Hanging out with friends and sharing an ice cold drink is what
summer is all about. This refreshing punch made with Cruzan®
Rum, Pinnacle® Vodka, DeKuyper® Blue Curacao and hints of
lemon and pineapple will be sure to cool you off all summer
long.

INGREDIENTS

½ part Cruzan® Coconut Rum
1 part Pinnacle® Original Vodka
½ part DeKuyper® Blue Curacao Liqueur
1 part Pineapple Juice
1 part Lemon Juice
Garnish:Lemon Wedge

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine ingredients with ice into a large bowl or
pitcher and stir. Garnish with a lemon wedge, add
paper straws and serve.
