HALF & HALF PUNCH

main spirit

Cordials
Rum

FLAVOR

Sweet

skill level

Beginner

Sweeten up your summer gathering with this refreshing crowd-
pleaser! Pair the classic combination of lemonade and iced tea
with smooth vodka and a twist of peach schnapps to make this
punch fit for any party.

INGREDIENTS

1 partPinnacle® Original Vodka
2 partsLemonade
2 partsIced Tea
Splash ofDeKuyper® Peachtree® Schnapps
LemonWedge garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine ingredients in a punch bowl or pitcher and
serve over ice.
