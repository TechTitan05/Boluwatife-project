ABOUT US

Nimee Cocktail is a craft cocktail company dedicated to
redefining the art of mixology. Our team of experienced
mixologist have crafted a comprehensive program to teach
you the fundamentals of mixology, from classic cocktails to
modern twists. We believe that anyone can become a skilled
mixologists with the right training and practice.

Our Story:

Founded by Ekundayo Boluwatife, a seasoned bartender and cocktail enthusiast, Nimee Cocktail was born out of a desire to inspire a new generation of mixologists, one cocktail at a time. Join our community, and discover the joy of crafting exceptional drinks that delight the sense and bring people together.Cheers!

Mission:

At Nimee Cocktail, we're passionate about empowering cocktail enthusiasts to become confident mixologist. Our mission is to provide hands-on training, expert guidance, and creative inspiration to help you master the art of craft cocktails.

Services:

  • Cocktail Classes: Join our interactive classes,covering topics like mixologgy basics, flavor pairing, and advanced techniques.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions focused on specific skkills, like molecular mixology or cocktail paring.
  • Private Events: Host your own cocktail-making party or corporate event with our expert guidance.
  • Online Ressources: Access our library of recipes, tutorials, and tips to continue learning at home.
