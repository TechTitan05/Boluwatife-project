SAUZA® BLUE CLASSIC MARGARITA

main spirit

Cordials
Rum

FLAVOR

Sweet

skill level

Beginner

Timeless and simple, there’s a reason classics never go out of
style. Pure blanco tequila, aromatic triple sec and zesty lime
make up the classic margarita recipe we’ve come to love. Pair it
with a salty or spicy dish for an explosion of flavors.

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 parts
Sauza® Blue 100% Agave Blanco Tequila
1 partLime Juice
1/2 partDeKuyper® Triple Sec Liqueur
1/2 partSimple Syrup
Garnish:Lime

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice
and shake until well chilled. Strain into a cocktail
glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a lime wheel, lime
wedge, or salt rim.

How Do I Make A Classic Fresh Margarita?

There’s a reason the classic margarita recipe is called a classic. It’s simple,
delicious and a crowd favorite. To create your own, just grab a cocktail shaker,
add 2 parts tequila, 1½ parts triple sec, 1 part lime juice and 1½ parts simple or
agave syrup.
